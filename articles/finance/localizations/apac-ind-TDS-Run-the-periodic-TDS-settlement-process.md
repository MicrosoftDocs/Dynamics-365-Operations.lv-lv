# <a name="run-the-periodic-tds-settlement-process"></a><span data-ttu-id="c64a5-103">Periodisko TDS norēķinu procesa palaišana</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-103">Run the periodic TDS settlement process</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="c64a5-104">Šajā tēmā skaidrots, kā nomaksāt periodisko No kopējās ienākumu summas atskaitīto nodokli (TDS).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-104">This topic explains how to settle periodic Tax Deducted at Source (TDS).</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-105">Atveriet **Nodoklis \> Deklarācijas \> Ieturētais nodoklis \> Ieturētā nodokļa maksājums**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-105">Go to **Tax \> Declarations \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax payment**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-106">[![Ieturētā nodokļa maksājuma dialoga lodziņš](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-106">[![Withholding tax payment dialog box](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-107">Dialoglodziņa **Ieturētā nodokļa maksājums** laukā **Nodokļa tips** atlasiet **TDS**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-107">In the **Withholding tax payment** dialog box, in the **Tax type** field, select **TDS**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-108">Laukā **Nodokļu konta numurs (TAN)** atlasiet Nodokļu konta numuru (TAN), lai izpildītu norēķinu procesu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-108">In the **Tax Account Number (TAN)** field, select the Tax Account Number (TAN) to run the settlement process for.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-109">Laukā **Ieturētā nodokļa komponentu grupa** atlasiet TDS sastāvdaļu grupu, lai palaistu norēķinu procesu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-109">In the **Withholding tax component group** field, select the TDS component group to run the settlement process for.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-110">Laukā **Norēķinu periods** atlasiet TDS norēķinu periodu, lai palaistu norēķinu procesu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-110">In the **Settlement period** field, select the TDS settlement period to run the settlement process for.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-111">TDS norēķinu process tiek izpildīts visiem periodiem, kas ir iestatīti TDS norēķinu periodam cilnes **Periodi** lapā **Ieturētā nodokļa norēķinu periodi** (**Nodoklis \> Netiešie nodokļi \> Ieturētais nodoklis \> Ieturētā nodokļa norēķinu periodi**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-111">The TDS settlement process is run for all periods that are set up for the TDS settlement period on the **Periods** tab of the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page (**Tax \> Indirect taxes \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax settlement periods**).</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-112">Laukā **sākuma datums** atlasiet sākuma datumu, no kā palaist TDS norēķinu procesu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-112">In the **From date** field, select the start date to run the TDS settlement process from.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-113">Noteiktam periodam norēķinu periodā sākuma datumu, kas definēts šim periodam, uzskata par “no” datumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-113">For a specific period within the settlement period, the start date that is defined for the period is taken as the "from" date.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-114">Piemēram, TDS norēķinu periodam ir divi periodi: 2009. gada 1. aprīļa līdz 30. aprīlim, un no 2009. gada 1. maija – 31. maijam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-114">For example, the TDS settlement period has two periods: April 1 through April 30, 2009, and May 1 through May 31, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-115">Ja atlasāt **06/04/2009** (2009. gada 6. aprīli) kā sākuma datumu laukā **sākuma datums**, norēķinu process joprojām darbosies no 2009. gada 1. aprīļa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-115">If you select **04/06/2009** (April 6, 2009) as the start date in the **From date** field, the settlement process will still run from April 1, 2009.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-116">Ja laukā **sākuma datums** ievadāt vēlāku periodu, bet nenosedzot iepriekšējo periodu norēķinu periodā, norēķinu periods neparādīsies nevienam iepriekšējam periodam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-116">If you enter a later period in the **From date** field, but without settling a previous period within the settlement period, the settlement won't occur for any previous periods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-117">Piemēram, TDS norēķinu periodam ir trīs periodi: 2009. gada 1. aprīlis līdz 30. aprīlis, 2009. gada 1. maijs līdz 31. maijs un 2009. gada 1. jūnijs līdz 30. jūnijs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-117">For example, the TDS settlement period has three periods: April 1 through April 30, 2009, May 1 through May 31, 2009, and June 1 through June 30, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-118">ja atlasāt **01/05/2009** (2009. gada 1. maijs) kā sākuma datumu laukā **sākuma datums** norēķinu process tiks veikts tikai no 2009. gada 1. maija līdz 31. maijam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-118">If you select **05/01/2009** (May 1, 2009) as the start date in the **From date** field, the settlement process will run only from May 1 through May 31, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-119">Norēķini nebūs no 2009. gada 1. aprīļa līdz 30. aprīlim.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-119">The settlement won't occur for April 1 through April 30, 2009.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-120">Laukā **Darījuma datums** atlasiet datumu, kad grāmatot TDS norēķinu darījumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-120">In the **Transaction date** field, select the date to post the TDS settlement transaction.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-121">Dialoglodziņa **Ieturētā nodokļa maksājuma versija** atlasiet TDS norēķinu versiju:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-121">In the **Withholding tax payment version** field, select the TDS settlement version:</span></span>

     - <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-122">**Sākotnējais** – izmantojiet šo opciju, lai palaistu TDS norēķinu procesu pirmo reizi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-122">**Original** – Use this option to run the TDS settlement process for the first time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-123">Sākotnējā maksājuma versija tiek izmantota tikai vienu reizi, lai palaistu TDS norēķinu procesu attiecībā uz TAN, ieturētā nodokļa komponenta grupas un ieturētā nodokļa norēķinu perioda kombināciju.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-123">The original payment version is used only one time to run the TDS settlement process for a combination of a TAN, a withholding tax component group, and a withholding tax settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-124">**Jaunākās versijas** – izmantojiet šo opciju, ja TDS norēķinu process jau ticis palaists norādītajam periodam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-124">**Latest versions** – Use this option if the TDS settlement process has already been run for the specified period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-125">Ietveriet atpakaļ datētu darījumu, kas tika grāmatotas pēc tam, kad perioda norēķinu process tika veikts iepriekš.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-125">Include back-dated transactions that were posted after the settlement process was previously run for the period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-126">Šo opciju var izmantot, lai norēķinu procesu palaistu vairākas reizes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-126">You can use this option to run the settlement process any number of times.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-127">Atzīmējiet izvēles rūtiņu **Atjaunināt** , lai palaistu TDS norēķinu procesu un grāmatotu summas virsgrāmatas kontos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-127">Select the **Update** check box to run the TDS settlement process and post the amounts to the ledger accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-128">Ja šī izvēles rūtiņa ir notīrīta, norēķinu process netiks palaists un finanšu ieraksti netiks ģenerēti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-128">If this check box is cleared, the settlement process won't be run, and the financial entries won't be generated.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-129">Atlasiet **Labi**, lai palaistu TDS norēķinu procesu un ģenerētu ieturētā nodokļa maksājuma pārskatu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-129">Select **OK** to run the TDS settlement process and generate the withholding tax payment report.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-130">To TDS darījumu statuss, kas ietvertas norēķinu procesā, tiek rādīts kā **Nosegts** laukā **Norēķini** (dodieties uz **Kreditori \> Maksājumi \> Kreditoru maksājumu žurnāls**, atlasiet **Rindas**, atlasiet **Funkcijas** un pēc tam atlasiet **Norēķini**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-130">The status of TDS transactions that are included in the settlement process is shown as **Settled** on the **Settlement** page (go to **Accounts payable \> Payments \> Vendor payment journal**, select **Lines**, select **Functions**, and then select **Settlement**).</span></span>

### <a name="important-points"></a><span data-ttu-id="c64a5-131">Svarīga informācija</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-131">Important points</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-132">Ja norēķinu procesa laikā nav atlasīta ieturētā nodokļa komponentu grupa, norēķini notiek visām ieturētā nodokļa komponentu grupām atlasītajā TAN un norēķinu periodā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-132">If a withholding tax component group isn't selected during the settlement process, the settlement occurs for all the withholding tax component groups for the selected TAN and settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-133">Katrai ieturētā nodokļa komponentu grupai lapā **Atvērt darījuma rediģēšanu** tiek izveidota atsevišķa rinda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-133">A separate line is created for each withholding tax component group on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-134">Norēķinu process balstās uz norēķinu perioda vērtētāja rakstura kategoriju.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-134">The settlement process is based on the nature of assessee category for a settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-135">Darījumi, kuros vērtētāja rakstura kategorija ir **Uzņēmums**, tiek norēķināti un tiek rādīti vienā rindā lapā **Atvērt darījuma rediģēšanu**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-135">Transactions where the nature of assessee category is **Company** are settled and are shown as one line on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-136">Darījumi, kuros vērtētāja raksturs ir kas cits, nevis **Uzņēmums**, tiek norēķināti un tiek rādīti vienā rindā lapā **Atvērt darbību rediģēšanu**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-136">Transactions where the nature of assessee is something other than **Company** are settled and are shown as one line on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-137">Apmaksāto TDS darījumu rindu izpildes termiņš lapā **Atvērt darbību rediģēšanu** ir balstīts uz maksājumu noteikumiem, kas TDS iestādes kreditoram ir noteikti lapā **Kreditori**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-137">The due date for the settled TDS transaction lines on the **Open transaction editing** page is based on the terms of payment that are defined for the TDS authority vendor on the **Vendors** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c64a5-138">Ja maksājumu nosacījumi nav definēti TDS iestādes kreditoram, apmaksas perioda pēdējā diena tiek parādīta kā apmaksas datums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c64a5-138">If the terms of payments aren't defined for the TDS authority vendor, the last day of the settlement period is shown as the due date.</span></span>
