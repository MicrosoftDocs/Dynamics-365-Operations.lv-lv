<span data-ttu-id="42df2-104">Šajā tēmā ir sniegts to normatīvi atjauninājumu saraksts, kas ir izlaisti Dynamics 365 Finance atbalstītajās lokalizācijās.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42df2-104">This topic lists the regulatory updates that are planned and released in Dynamics 365 Finance supported localizations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42df2-105">Piegādes termiņi var tikt mainīti, un plānotā funkcionalitāte var būt atšķirīga vai netikt izlaista.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42df2-105">Delivery timelines might change, and projected functionality might be different or might not be released.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42df2-106">Papildinformāciju lasiet [Microsoft politikā](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=2007332).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42df2-106">For more information, see [Microsoft policy](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=2007332).</span></span>
