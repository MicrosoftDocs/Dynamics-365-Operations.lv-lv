# <a name="customer-payment-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="35298-103">Debitoru maksājumu pārskats</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-103">Customer payment overview</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="35298-104">Šis uzdevumu ceļvedis palīdzēs izprast dažādas metodes, kuras izmanto, lai ievadītu debitora maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-104">This task guide walks through various methods used to enter customer payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-105">Šajā uzdevumā tiek izmantots demonstrācijas uzņēmums USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-105">This task uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="35298-106">Pārejiet uz sadaļu **Navigācijas rūts > Moduļi > Debitori > Maksājumi > Maksājumu žurnāls**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-106">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Accounts receivable > Payments > Payment journal**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="35298-108">Atlasiet maksājumu žurnālu, kurā tiks saglabāti debitoru maksājumi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-108">Select the payment journal which the customer payments will be saved.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="35298-109">Atlasiet žurnālu vai ievadiet to manuāli.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-109">Select or manually enter the journal.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="35298-113">Ievadiet debitoru, no kura saņēmāt maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-113">Enter the customer from whom you received the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-114">Ja nezināt debitoru, bet zināt apmaksāto transakciju, lai ievadītu maksājumu, var izmantot lauku Transakcija.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-114">If you don't know the customer but do know a transaction that was paid, you can use the Transaction field to enter the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-115">Ievadiet vai atlasiet rēķinu laukā Transakcija.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-115">Enter or select the invoice in the Transaction field.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-116">Kad atlasīsiet transakciju, debitors automātiski parādīsies pēc noklusējuma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-116">The customer will automatically default after you select the transaction.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="35298-117">Laukā **Maksājuma atsauce** ievadiet maksājuma atsauci.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-117">In the **Payment reference** field, enter a payment reference.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-118">Maksājuma atsauce var būt debitora čeka numurs vai atsauce no klienta elektroniskā maksājuma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-118">The payment reference could be the customer's check number or a reference from the customer's electronic payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-119">Maksājuma atsauce ir nepieciešama tikai tad, ja atzīmējat maksājumu iekļaušanai depozīta kvītī.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-119">The payment reference is only required if you mark to include the payment on a deposit slip.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="35298-120">Laukā **Depozīta kvīts** atlasiet, vai maksājums tiks iekļauts depozīta kvītī.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-120">In the **Deposit slip** field, select whether the payment will be included on a deposit slip.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="35298-121">Laukā **Summa** ievadiet debitora maksājuma summu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-121">In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of the customer payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-122">Maksājuma summa pēc noklusējuma neparādīsies.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-122">The payment amount will not default.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-123">Tā jāievada manuāli.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-123">It must be manually entered.</span></span> 
10. <span data-ttu-id="35298-124">Atzīmējiet rēķinus, kurus apmaksāja debitors.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-124">Mark the invoices that were paid by the customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-125">Ja maksājums ir priekšapmaksa, jums nav nepieciešams atzīmēt rēķinus nosegšanai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-125">If the payment is a prepayment, you are not required to mark any invoices for settlement.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-126">Maksājumu joprojām var saglabāt un iegrāmatot.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-126">The payment can still be saved and posted.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-127">Rēķina segšana var notikt vēlāk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-127">Settlement to an invoice can happen at a later time.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="35298-128">Ievadiet maksājuma summu, kura jāsedz atzīmētajam rēķinam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-128">Enter the amount of the payment that should be settled to the marked invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-129">Šo lauku var izmantot, kad maksājums ir daļējs maksājums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-129">This field can be used when the payment is for a partial payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-130">Ja neievadāt summu, nosedzamā summa tiks noteikta automātiski.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-130">If you don't enter an amount, the amount to settle will automatically be determined for you.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="35298-131">Noklikšķiniet uz **Saglabāt žurnālā**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-131">Click **Save in journal**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-132">Pirms saglabājat maksājumu žurnālā, pārliecinieties, vai ir definēts korespondējošais konts.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-132">Before you save the payment to the journal, make sure that the offset account is defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-133">Izmantojot funkciju **Saglabāt žurnālā**, maksājums tiks saglabāts un pārvietots uz žurnālu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-133">Using **Save in journal** will save the payment and move it to the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-134">Pēc tam var turpināt ievadīt nākamo maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-134">You can then continue entering the next payment.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="35298-135">Aizvērt lapu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-135">Close the page.</span></span>
14. <span data-ttu-id="35298-136">**Darbību rūtī** noklikšķiniet uz Rindas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-136">On **Action pane**, click Lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-137">Atverot Rindas, ieraudzīsiet visus maksājumus, kas ierakstīti lapā **Ievadīt debitora maksājumus** un saglabāti žurnālā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-137">When opening Lines, you will see any payments you recorded on the **Enter customer payments** page and saved into the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-138">Šo lapu var arī izmantot, lai ievadītu jaunos debitoru maksājumus vai rediģēt esošos debitora maksājumus pirms to grāmatošanas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-138">You can also use this page to enter new customer payments, or edit existing customer payment before they are posted.</span></span>
15. <span data-ttu-id="35298-139">Noklikšķiniet uz **Jauns**, lai izveidotu citu maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-139">Click **New** to create another payment.</span></span> 
16. <span data-ttu-id="35298-140">Atlasiet debitoru, no kura saņēmāt maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-140">Select the customer from whom you received the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-141">Ja nezināt debitoru, bet zināt ar maksājumu apmaksāto rēķinu, izmantojiet lauku Rēķins, lai manuāli ievadītu vai atlasītu rēķinu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-141">If you don't know the customer but know an invoice paid by the payment, use the Invoice field to manually enter or select the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-142">Debitors parādīsies pēc noklusējuma, kad tiks atlasīts rēķins.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-142">The customer will default after the invoice is selected.</span></span>  
17. <span data-ttu-id="35298-143">Noklikšķiniet **Transakciju nosegšana**, lai atzīmētu apmaksātos rēķinus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-143">Click **Settle transactions** to mark invoices that were paid.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-144">Jums nav nepieciešams segt rēķinu maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-144">You are not required to settle the payment to any invoices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-145">Ja tā ir priekšapmaksa vai ja nezināt, kāds rēķins tiek apmaksāts, varat ievadīt un iegrāmatot maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-145">If this is a prepayment or if you don't know what invoice was paid, you can enter and post the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-146">Ar maksājumu var nosegt rēķinu vēlāk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-146">The payment can be settled to an invoice at a later point.</span></span>  
18. <span data-ttu-id="35298-147">Atzīmējiet rēķinus, kas apmaksāti ar šo maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-147">Mark the invoices paid by the payment.</span></span> 
19. <span data-ttu-id="35298-148">Laukā **Summa** ievadiet maksājuma summu, kura tiks segta šim rēķinam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-148">In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of the payment that will be settled to the invoice.</span></span>
20. <span data-ttu-id="35298-149">Noklikšķiniet uz **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-149">Click **OK**.</span></span>
21. <span data-ttu-id="35298-150">Laukā **Maksājuma atsauce** ievadiet maksājuma atsauci.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-150">In the **Payment reference** field, enter a payment reference.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35298-151">Maksājuma atsauce ir nepieciešama tikai tad, ja atzīmējat maksājumu iekļaušanai depozīta kvītī.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-151">The payment reference is only required if you mark to include the payment on a deposit slip.</span></span>  
22. <span data-ttu-id="35298-152">Lai grāmatotu starpuzņēmumu debitora rēķinu, sadaļā **Darbību rūts** noklikšķiniet uz **Grāmatot**, lai iegrāmatotu debitoru maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35298-152">On **Action pane**, click **Post** to post the customer payments.</span></span>
