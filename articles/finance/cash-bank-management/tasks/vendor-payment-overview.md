# <a name="vendor-payment-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="83f84-103">Kreditoru maksājumu pārskats</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-103">Vendor payment overview</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="83f84-104">Šis uzdevumu ceļvedis palīdzēs izprast dažādas metodes, ko izmanto, lai izveidotu kreditoru maksājumus, ieskaitot to, kā izmantot maksājuma priekšlikumu vai manuāli ievadīt vienreizēju maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-104">This task guide will walk you through various methods used to create vendor payments, including how to use a payment proposal or manually entering a one-off payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-105">Procedūrā tiek izmantoti demonstrācijas uzņēmuma “USMF” dati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-105">This procedure uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-106">Pārejiet uz sadaļu **Navigācijas rūts > Moduļi > Kreditori > Maksājumi > Maksājumu žurnāls**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-106">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Accounts payable > Payments > Payment journal**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-107">Klikšķiniet **Jauns**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-108">Atlasiet maksājumu žurnālu, kurā jāsaglabā kreditoru maksājumi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-108">Select the payment journal in which to save the vendor payments.</span></span> 
4. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-109">Atlasiet žurnālu vai ievadiet to manuāli.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-109">Select the journal or manually enter it.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-110">Noklikšķiniet uz **Rindas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-110">Click **Lines**.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-111">**Darbību rūtī** noklikšķiniet uz **Maksājuma priekšlikums**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-111">On **Ation pane**, click **Payment proposal**.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-112">Noklikšķiniet uz **Izveidot maksājuma priekšlikumu**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-112">Click **Create payment proposal**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-113">Maksājuma priekšlikums ir vaicājums, ko izmanto, lai atlasītu rēķinus apmaksai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-113">The payment proposal is a query used to select invoices for payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-114">Maksājamo rēķinu sarakstu var rediģēt pirms kreditoru maksājumu izveides vai ģenerēšanas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-114">You can edit the list of invoices to pay before creating or generating the vendor payments.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-115">Atlasiet rēķinus apmaksai pēc izpildes datuma, termiņatlaides vai abus tipus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-115">Select invoices for payment by due date, cash discount, or both.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-116">Ievadiet datumu salīdzināšanai ar izpildes datumu vai termiņatlaidi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-116">Enter the date for comparing to the due date or cash discount.</span></span> 
10. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-117">Nav obligāti: ievadiet visiem maksājumiem izmantojamo maksājuma datumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-117">Optional: Enter a payment date used for all payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-118">Šeit ievadītais datums tiks uzskatīts par maksājuma datumu visiem izveidotajiem maksājumiem neatkarīgi no apmaksas datuma vai termiņatlaides datuma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-118">The date entered here will be the payment date for all payments created, regardless of the due date or cash discount date.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-119">Nav obligāti: ievadiet minimālo maksājuma datumu, ko var izmantot kā maksājuma datumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-119">Optional: Enter a minimum payment date which may be used as the payment date.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-120">Agrākais maksājuma datums būs agrākais datums, ko izmanto, veidojot maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-120">The minimum payment date will be the earliest date used when creating payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-121">Piemēram, ja rēķina apmaksas datums ir agrākais maksājuma datums, par maksājuma datumu kļūs apmaksas datums nevis agrākais maksājuma datums, lai apmaksātu rēķinu pēdējā iespējamā datumā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-121">For example, if an invoice has a due date after the minimum payment date, the due date will become the payment date instead of the minimum payment date in order to pay the invoice on the latest possible date.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-122">Ievadiet vaicājuma papildu ierobežojumus sadaļā **Iekļaujamie ieraksti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-122">Enter additional query restrictions under **Records to include** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-123">Filtru bieži lieto, lai ierobežotu apmaksai atlasītos rēķinus pēc kreditoru grupas vai maksāšanas metodes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-123">The filter is often used to restrict the invoices selected for payment by vendor group or method of payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-124">Piemēram, var pievienot filtru, lai šajā maksājumu izpildē apmaksātu rēķinus tikai ar čeku.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-124">For example, you may add a filter to only pay invoices by check in this pay run.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-125">Ievadiet vaicājuma papildu ierobežojumu vai maksājuma noklusējuma iestatījumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-125">Enter additional query restriction or payment defaults.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-126">Papildu parametrus var izmantot, lai definētu maksājuma valūtu vai iespējotu šai maksājumu izpildei centralizētos maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-126">The additional parameters can be used to define the payment currency or to enable centralized payments for this pay run.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-127">Noklikšķiniet uz **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-127">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-128">Noklikšķinot uz **Labi**, parādīsies vaicājuma rezultāti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-128">After clicking **OK**, the results of the query will appear.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-129">Ja nevēlaties priekšskatīt apmaksai atlasīto rēķinu sarakstu, var atgriezties uz kopsavilkuma cilni **Parametri** un mainīt iestatījumu **Izveidot maksājumus bez rēķina priekšskatīšanas** uz "Jā".</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-129">If you don't want to preview the list of invoices selected to pay, you can go back to the **Parameters** fast tab and change the setting **Create payments without invoice preview** to "Yes".</span></span>  
15. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-130">Izvēlēties pogu **Rādīt maksājuma pārskatu**, lai apskatītu maksājumus, kas tiks izveidoti kreditoram saistībā ar atlasīto rēķinu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-130">Choose the **Show payment overview** button to view the payments that will be created for the vendor on the invoice selected.</span></span>
16. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-131">Izvēlēties pogu **Paslēpt maksājuma pārskatu**, lai paslēptu maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-131">Choose the **Hide payment overview** button to hide the payments.</span></span> 
17. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-132">Noklikšķiniet uz **Izveidot maksājumus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-132">Click **Create payments**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-133">Pirms izvēlaties **Izveidot maksājumus**, ar peles labo pogu var noklikšķināt uz režģa un eksportēt rēķinu sarakstu uz Excel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-133">Before choosing **Create payments**, you can right click on the grid and export the list of invoices to Excel.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-134">Poga **Izveidot maksājumu** izveidos kreditoru maksājumus maksājumu žurnālā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-134">The **Create payments** button will create the vendor payments in the payment journal.</span></span>  
18. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-135">Ieskenējiet savus maksājumus un pārliecinieties, vai maksāšanas metode ir norādīta visiem maksājumiem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-135">Scan your payments and make sure the method of payment is defined for all payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-136">Ja ģenerējat tādus maksājumus, kā čeka drukāšana vai elektroniskā maksājuma izveide, jānosaka maksāšanas metode.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-136">If you generate the payments, such as printing a check or creating an electronic payment, the method of payment must be defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-137">Maksāšanas tips arī noteiks noklusējuma bankas kontu, no kura tiks veikts maksājums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-137">The method of payment will also default the bank account from the payment will be made.</span></span>  
19. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-138">Noklikšķiniet uz **Jauns**, lai izveidotu vienreizēju maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-138">Click **New** to create a one-off payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-139">Vienreizējs maksājums var tikt pievienots maksājumu žurnālam jebkurā laikā pirms grāmatošanas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-139">A one-off payment can be added to a payment journal at any time prior to posting.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-140">To var izdarīt, noklikšķinot uz pogas **Jauns** un manuāli pievienojot maksājuma informāciju, nevis izmantojot **Maksājuma priekšlikumu**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-140">This is done by clicking the **New** button and adding the payment information manually, rather than using the **Payment proposal**.</span></span>  
20. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-141">Atlasiet kreditoru, kuram tiks veikts maksājums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-141">Select the vendor to whom the payment will be made.</span></span>
21. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-142">Ja pastāv rēķins apmaksai, atlasiet **Transakciju nosegšana**, lai atlasītu apmaksājamo rēķinu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-142">If an invoice exists to pay, select **Settle transactions** to select the invoice for payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-143">Ja tā ir priekšapmaksa, šī darbība nav obligāta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-143">If this is a prepayment, this step is optional.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-144">Maksājumu var izveidot, neatlasot rēķinu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-144">You can create the payment without selecting any invoice.</span></span> 
22. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-145">Atzīmējiet visus rēķinus, kas tiks apmaksāti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-145">Mark any invoices that will be paid.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-146">Ja apmaksājamo rēķinu atlasīšanai izmantojat funkciju **Transakciju nosegšana**, maksājuma summa tiks automātiski aprēķināta, pamatojoties uz rēķiniem, kurus atzīmējāt apmaksai, un summu, kuru ievadījāt laukā **Nosedzamā summa**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-146">If you use the **Settle transactions** to select the invoices for payment, the payment amount will automatically be calculated based on what invoices you mark for payment, and what amount you enter in the **Amount to settle**.</span></span>
23. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-147">Noklikšķiniet uz **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-147">Click **OK**.</span></span>
24. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-148">Ja vēlaties dzēst maksājumu, atzīmējiet rindu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-148">If you want to delete a payment, mark the row.</span></span>
25. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-149">Noklikšķiniet uz **Dzēst**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-149">Click **Delete**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-150">Dzēšot maksājumu, tiks izdzēsts tikai konkrētais maksājums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-150">Deleting a payment will only delete the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-151">Visi apmaksai atzīmētie rēķini joprojām būs pieejami apmaksai ar citu maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-151">Any invoices marked for payment will still be available to be paid by another payment.</span></span>
26. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-152">Noklikšķiniet uz pogas **Jā**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-152">Click **Yes**.</span></span>
27. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-153">Izvēlieties **Ģenerēt maksājumu**, lai drukātu čekus vai izveidotu elektroniskā maksājuma failu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-153">Choose **Generate payment** to print checks or create the electronic payment file.</span></span>
28. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-154">Atlasiet maksājuma metodi, kuru vēlaties izveidot.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-154">Select the method of payment that you want to generate.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-155">Maksājumu žurnāls var saturēt gan čeku maksājumus, gan elektroniskos maksājumus, taču vienlaikus var izveidot tikai viena veida maksājumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-155">The payment journal can contain payments for both Checks and electronic payments, but you can only generate one payment type at a time.</span></span>
29. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-156">Atlasiet bankas kontu, no kura ģenerēt maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-156">Select the bank account from which to generate the payments.</span></span>
30. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-157">Noklikšķiniet uz **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-157">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83f84-158">Maksājumi tiks izveidoti tikai maksājumiem, kas atbilst atlasītajai **Maksājuma metodei** un **Bankas kontam**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-158">Payments will only be generated for payments that match the **Method of payment** and **Bank account** you selected.</span></span>
31. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-159">Ja veidojat **Čeku maksājumus**, izvēlieties **Dokuments**, lai nodrošinātu, ka čekiem tiek izmantots pareizais drukas galamērķis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-159">If you are generating **Checks**, choose **Document** to ensure the correct print destination for the checks.</span></span>
32. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-160">Noklikšķiniet uz **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-160">Click **OK**.</span></span>
33. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-161">Noklikšķiniet uz **Labi**, lai izveidotu maksājumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-161">Click **OK** to generate the payments.</span></span>
34. <span data-ttu-id="83f84-162">Noklikšķiniet uz **Grāmatot**, ja visi maksājumi ir apstiprināti un ģenerēti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83f84-162">Click **Post** if all the payments are approved and generated.</span></span>
