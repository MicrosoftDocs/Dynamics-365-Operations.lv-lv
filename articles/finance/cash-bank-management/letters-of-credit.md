# <a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-103">Akreditīvi</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-103">Letters of credit</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-104">Akreditīvi ir bankas dokumenti, kas parasti tiek lietoti pārrobežu preču pirkšanai un pārdošanai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-105">Akreditīvi tiek izmantoti starptautiskajām transakcijām, lai nodrošinātu, ka maksājumi tiks veikti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-106">Akreditīvs ir līgums, kuru izsniedz banka un kurā banka piekrīt veikt maksājumu pircēja vārdā, ja tiek izpildīti starp pircēju un pārdevēju noslēgtā līguma nosacījumi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-107">Ņemiet vērā, ka akreditīvu dēvē arī par dokumentāro akreditīvu (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-108">Importa akreditīva gadījumā juridiskā persona ir pircējs vai akreditīva pieteicējs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-109">Eksporta akreditīva gadījumā juridiskā persona ir pārdevējs vai akreditīva saņēmējs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-110">Ar akreditīvu ir saistītas šādas puses:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-111">pieteicējs (pircējs), kurš plāno maksāt par precēm;</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-112">saņēmējs (pārdevējs), kurš saņems maksājumu;</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-113">izdevēja banka, kas izsniedz kredītvēstuli;</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-114">paziņotājbanka, kas izpilda transakciju pieteicēja vārdā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-115">Akreditīvs ietver preču aprakstu, visus nepieciešamos dokumentus, nosūtīšanas datumu un beigu datumu, līdz kuram ir jāveic maksājums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-116">Izdevēja banka iekasē peļņu par akreditīvu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-117">Akreditīvs var būt atsaucams vai neatsaucams.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-118">Akreditīva raksturs var būt nododams, nenododams vai atjaunojams.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c2bb2-119">Parasti akreditīvs ir neatsaucams un akceptēts līgums, ka maksājums tiks veikts noteiktam saņēmējam pēc pilnīgas un precīzas nosūtīšanas dokumentācijas iesniegšanas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bb2-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
