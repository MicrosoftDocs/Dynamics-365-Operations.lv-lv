5. <span data-ttu-id="0760e-112">Atlasiet lauka Stunda vērtību Jā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0760e-112">Select Yes in the Hour field.</span></span>

10. <span data-ttu-id="0760e-117">Sarakstā atzīmējiet atlasīto rindu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0760e-117">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span>
