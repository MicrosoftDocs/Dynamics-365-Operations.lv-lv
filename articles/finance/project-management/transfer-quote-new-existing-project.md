# <a name="transfer-a-quotation-to-a-project"></a><span data-ttu-id="f5778-103">Piedāvājuma pārsūtīšana uz projektu</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f5778-103">Transfer a quotation to a project</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="f5778-104">Kad projekta piedāvājuma statuss ir **Apstiprināts**, varat pārsūtīt dažas vai visas piedāvājuma rindas uz jaunu vai esošu projektu, izmantojot vedni **Pārsūtīt uz projektu**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f5778-104">When a project quotation has a status of **Confirmed**, you can transfer some or all the quotation lines to a new or existing project by using the **Transfer to project** wizard.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="f5778-105">Lai izmantotu vedni, veiciet sekojošās darbības.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f5778-105">To use the wizard, follow these steps.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="f5778-106">Dodieties uz **Projektu vadība un uzskaite** > **Piedāvājumi** > **Projekta piedāvājumi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f5778-106">Go to **Project management and accounting** > **Quotations** > **Project quotations**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="f5778-107">Atlasiet piedāvājumu, kuru vēlaties pārsūtīt, un cilnes **Sekošana** grupā **Modificēt** atlasiet **Pārsūtīt uz projektu**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f5778-107">Select the quotation you want to transfer and on the **Follow up** tab, in the **Modify** group, select **Transfer to project**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="f5778-108">Sekojiet vednim **Pārsūtīt piedāvājumam uz projektu**, lai sasaistītu un pārsūtītu piedāvājumu ar projektu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f5778-108">Follow the **Transfer quotation to project** wizard to associate and transfer the quotation with a project.</span></span>
