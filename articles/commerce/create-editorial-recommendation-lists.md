## <a name="create-a-new-list"></a><span data-ttu-id="963d8-106">Jauna saraksta izveide</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-106">Create a new list</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="963d8-107">Lai izveidotu pārvaldītu preču rekomendāciju sarakstu, veiciet šādas darbības.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-107">To create a curated product recommendation list, follow these steps.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-108">Pärejiet uz **Mazumtirdzniecība un komercija &gt; Preču ieteikumi &gt; Ieteikumu saraksti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-108">Go to **Retail and Commerce &gt; Product recommendations &gt; Recommendation lists**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-109">Atlasiet **Jauns**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-109">Select **New**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-110">Ievadiet vērtību laukā **Saraksta ID**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-110">In the **List Id** field, enter a value.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-111">Ievadiet vērtību laukā **Saraksta nosaukums**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-111">In the **List name** field, enter a value.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="963d8-112">**Saraksta nosaukums** ir tā saraksta nosaukums, kas parādīsies moduļa **Preču kolekcijas** pārraudzītu sarakstu sadaļā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-112">The **List name** is the title of the list that will appear in the curated lists section of the **Product collection** module.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-113">Lai pievienotu preces sarakstam , atlasiet **Pievienot preces**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-113">To add products to the list, select **Add products**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-114">Lai sarakstā mainītu preču secību, ievadiet vērtību kolonnā **Rādīšanas secība**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-114">To change the order of the products in the list, enter a value in the **Display order** column.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="963d8-115">Ja divām precēm ir tāda pati rādīšanas secības vērtība, tad šo divu rezultātu gala secība var atšķirties no tās, kas ir operāciju uzskaites daļā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-115">If two products have the same display order value, then the final order of those two results may differ from the back office.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="963d8-116">Atlasiet **Saglabāt**, lai saglabātu sarakstu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="963d8-116">Select **Save** to save the list.</span></span>
