## <a name="create-a-hierarchy"></a><span data-ttu-id="45eba-112">Izveidot hierarhiju</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-112">Create a hierarchy</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="45eba-113">Ejiet uz **Navigācijas rūts > Moduļi > Organizācijas administrēšana > Organizācijas > Organizācijas hierarhijas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-113">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Organization administration > Organizations > Organization hierarchies**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="45eba-115">Laukā **Nosaukums** ierakstiet kādu vērtību.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-115">In the **Name** field, type a value.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="45eba-117">Sarakstā atrodiet un atlasiet vajadzīgo ierakstu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-117">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45eba-118">Atlasiet nolūku, kas tiks piešķirts jūsu organizācijas hierarhijai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-118">Select a purpose to assign to your organization hierarchy.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="45eba-120">Sarakstā atzīmējiet atlasīto rindu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-120">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45eba-121">Atrodiet tikko izveidoto hierarhiju.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45eba-121">Find the hierarchy you just created.</span></span>
