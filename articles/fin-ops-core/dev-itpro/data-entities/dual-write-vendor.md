<span data-ttu-id="31456-104">Termins *Kreditors* attiecas uz piegādātāja organizāciju vai vienīgo īpašnieku, kas ir piegādes ķēdes procesa daļa un kas piegādā preces uzņēmumam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31456-104">The term *vendor* refers to a supplier organization or a sole proprietor that is part of the supply chain process, and that supplies goods for the business.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31456-105">Kaut arī *kreditors* ir reģistrēts koncepts Finance and Operations programmās, kreditora koncepts nepastāv citās Dynamics 365 programmās.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31456-105">Although *vendor* is an established concept in Finance and Operations apps, a vendor concept doesn't exist in other Dynamics 365 apps.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31456-106">Tā vietā daži uzņēmumi pārslogo Konta elementu, lai uzglabātu gan debitora informāciju, gan kreditora informāciju.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31456-106">Instead, some businesses overload the Account entity to store both customer information and vendor information.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31456-107">Citi uzņēmumi izmanto pielāgotu kreditoru konceptu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31456-107">Other businesses use a custom vendor concept.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31456-108">Common Data Service integrācija atbalsta abus šos modeļus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31456-108">Common Data Service integration supports both these designs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31456-109">Tāpēc atkarībā no jūsu biznesa varat iespējot jebkuru no modeļiem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31456-109">Therefore, you can enable either of the designs, depending on your business scenario.</span></span>
