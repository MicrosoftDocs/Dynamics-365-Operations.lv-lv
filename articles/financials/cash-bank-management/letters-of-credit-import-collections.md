[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-105">Šajā rakstā ir sniegta vispārīga informācija par akreditīviem un importa inkaso.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-105">This article provides general information about letters of credit and import collections.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-106">Abu tipu bankas dokumenti bieži tiek lietoti pārrobežu preču pirkšanai un pārdošanai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-106">Both types of bank document are often used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span>

<a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="a66e6-107">Akreditīvi</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-107">Letters of credit</span></span>
-----------------

<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-108">Akreditīvi tiek izmantoti starptautiskajām transakcijām, un tie palīdz garantēt maksājumu veikšanu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-108">Letters of credit are used for international transactions and help guarantee that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-109">Akreditīvs ir bankas izsniegts līgums, kurā banka piekrīt garantēt maksājumu pircēja vārdā, ja tiek izpildīti starp pircēju un pārdevēju noslēgtā līguma nosacījumi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-109">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to guarantee payment on behalf of a buyer, provided that the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-110">Akreditīvs tiek saukts arī par dokumentāro akreditīvu (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-110">A letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-111">Importa akreditīva gadījumā juridiskā persona ir pircējs vai akreditīva pieteicējs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-111">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-112">Eksporta akreditīva gadījumā juridiskā persona ir pārdevējs vai akreditīva saņēmējs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-112">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-113">Ar kredītvēstuli ir saistītas šādas personas:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-113">The following parties are involved in a letter of credit:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-114">pieteicējs (pircējs), kurš plāno maksāt par precēm;</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-114">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-115">saņēmējs (pārdevējs), kurš saņems maksājumu;</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-115">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-116">izdevēja banka, kas izsniedz kredītvēstuli;</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-116">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-117">paziņotājbanka, kas izpilda transakciju pieteicēja vārdā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-117">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-118">Kredītvēstule ietver preču aprakstu, visus nepieciešamos dokumentus, nosūtīšanas datumu un beigu datumu, līdz kuram ir jāveic maksājums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-118">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment won't be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-119">Izdevēja banka iekasē peļņu par akreditīvu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-119">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-120">Kredītvēstule var būt **atsaucama** vai **neatsaucama**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-120">A letter of credit can be **revocable** or **irrevocable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-121">Kredītvēstules raksturs var būt **nododama**, **-nenododama** vai **atjaunojama**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-121">The nature of a letter of credit can be **transferable**, **non-transferable**, or **revolving**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-122">Parasti akreditīvs ir neatsaucams un akceptēts līgums, ka maksājums tiks veikts noteiktam saņēmējam pēc pilnīgas un precīzas nosūtīšanas dokumentācijas iesniegšanas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-122">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>

## <a name="import-collections"></a><span data-ttu-id="a66e6-123">Importa inkaso</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-123">Import collections</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-124">Importa inkaso ir līgums starp banku un eksportētāju (pārdevēju), kurā banka piekrīt piegādāt sūtījuma dokumentāciju starptautiskajam importētājam (pircējam).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-124">An import collection is an agreement between the bank and the exporter (seller), in which the bank agrees to deliver the shipping documentation to the international importer (buyer).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a66e6-125">Sūtījuma dokumentāciju banka piegādā, saņemot maksājumu par nosūtāmajām precēm skaidrā naudā vai saņemot parakstītu maksājuma uzdevumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-125">The bank is expected to deliver the shipping documentation upon receipt of payment for the shipped goods in cash, or upon receipt of a signed draft toward the payment.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="a66e6-126">Importa inkaso palīdz nodrošināt, ka pārdevējam tiek samaksāts, kad pircējs saņem sūtījuma dokumentus, lai veiktu importēto preču piegādi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a66e6-126">An import collection helps guarantee that the seller is paid when the buyer collects the shipping documents to take delivery of the imported goods.</span></span>
