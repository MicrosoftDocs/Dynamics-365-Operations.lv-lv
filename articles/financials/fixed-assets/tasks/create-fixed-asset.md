[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="31acf-104">Šis uzdevuma ceļvedis izmanto USMF demonstrācijas uzņēmumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-104">This task guide uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="31acf-105">Tajā tiks izveidots jauns pamatlīdzeklis, izmantojot Pamatlīdzekļu saraksta lapu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-105">It will create a new fixed asset using the Fixed asset list page.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-106">Pārejiet uz sadaļu Pamatlīdzekļi > Pamatlīdzekļi > Pamatlīdzekļi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-106">Go to Fixed assets > Fixed assets > Fixed assets.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-107">Noklikšķiniet uz Jauns.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-107">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-108">Ievadiet vai atlasiet vērtību laukā Pamatlīdzekļu grupa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-108">In the Fixed asset group field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="31acf-109">Ja pamatlīdzekļu parametros un pamatlīdzekļu grupā ir iespējota automātiska pamatlīdzekļu kodu piešķiršanas funkcionalitāte, numurs tiks piešķirts pēc noklusējuma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-109">The Number will default if you have enabled Autonumber fixed assets functionality in the Fixed assets parameters and the Fixed asset group.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="31acf-110">Ja tā nav, Jums jāievada unikāls kods pamatlīdzekļa identificēšanai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-110">If not, you must enter a unique number to identify the fixed asset.</span></span>

    * <span data-ttu-id="31acf-112">Ievadiet papildu informāciju, kas jūsu uzņēmumam ir nepieciešama par šo līdzekli.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-112">Enter the additional information that your business needs for this asset.</span></span>  
5. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-113">Noklikšķiniet uz Grāmatas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-113">Click Books.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-114">Ievadiet datumu laukā Iegādes datums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-114">In the Acquisition date field, enter a date.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-115">Ievadiet skaitli laukā Iegādes cena.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-115">In the Acquisition price field, enter a number.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="31acf-116">Ievadiet papildinformāciju, kas jūsu uzņēmumam ir nepieciešama par šo grāmatu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-116">Enter the additional information that your business needs for this book.</span></span>  
    * <span data-ttu-id="31acf-117">Ievadiet papildinformāciju, kas jūsu uzņēmumam ir nepieciešama par atlikušajām grāmatām.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-117">Enter the additional information that your business needs for the remaining books.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="31acf-118">Aizvērt lapu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31acf-118">Close the page.</span></span>
