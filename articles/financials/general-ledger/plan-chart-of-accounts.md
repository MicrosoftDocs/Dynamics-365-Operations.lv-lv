[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="54914-104">Šajā rakstā ir sniegta informācija, kas jums palīdzēs savai organizācijai plānot kontu plānu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-104">This article provides information that will help you plan the chart of accounts for your organization.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="54914-105">Lai organizācijā izsekotu un uzturētu finanšu informāciju, varat iestatīt kontu plānu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-105">To track and maintain financial information in an organization, you can set up a chart of accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-106">Kontu plāns ir kontu kopa, kas definē finanšu shēmu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-106">A chart of accounts is a collection of accounts that define a financial framework.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-107">Lai turpmāk izsekotu šo kontu transakcijas, varat pievienot segmentus, kurus sauc par finanšu dimensijām.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-107">To further track the transactions in these accounts, you can add segments, which are known as financial dimensions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-108">Piemēram, izdevumu kontā var iekļaut šādas finanšu dimensijas: Nodaļa, Izmaksu centrs un Nolūks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-108">For example, an expense account might include financial dimensions that are named Department, Cost center, and Purpose.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-109">Lietotāja definētajās kārtulas, ko sauc par konta struktūrām un papildu kārtulām, nosaka to, kā šīs finanšu dimensijas tiek pievienotas galvenajiem kontiem un citām finanšu dimensijām, kā arī to, kā var ievadīt transakcijas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-109">User-defined rules, which are known as account structures and advanced rules, determine how financial dimensions are attached to the main accounts and other financial dimensions, and also how transactions are entered.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="54914-110">Kontu plāns ir juridiskas personas Virsgrāmatas kontu strukturētais saraksts.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-110">The chart of accounts is a structured list of a legal entity’s general ledger accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-111">Saraksts tiek lietots, lai sagatavotu finanšu pārskatus iestādēm un īpašniekiem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-111">The list is used to prepare financial reports for authorities and owners.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-112">Konti tiek grupēti kontu veicos un pēc tam apkopoti lielākās kategorijās.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-112">The accounts are grouped into types of accounts and then further aggregated into larger categories.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-113">Vispārīgā līmenī konti tiek grupēti kā ieņēmumi un izmaksas (operāciju konti) un kā aktīvi un pasīvi (bilances konti).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-113">At the most general level, the accounts are grouped as revenues and costs (operating accounts), and assets and liabilities (balance accounts).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="54914-114">Kontu plānu var kopīgot un jebkura juridiskā persona organizācijā to var izmantot.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-114">A chart of accounts can be shared and used by any legal entity in an organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-115">Kontu plāns, ko izmanto juridiskā persona, tiek definēts lapā **Virsgrāmata**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-115">The chart of accounts that is used by a legal entity is defined on the **Ledger** page.</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="54914-117">Valsts/reģiona, kurā ir bāzēta jūsu organizācija, atskaišu prasības</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-117">The reporting requirements of the country/region where your organization is based</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="54914-118">Juridiskās personas prasības atskaišu veidošanai</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-118">The reporting requirements of your legal entity</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="54914-119">Nepieciešamā specifikācijas pakāpe ārējās organizācijās un savā organizācijā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-119">The degree of specification that is required, both for both external organizations and for your organization</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="54914-120">Kontu plānu var izveidot lapā **Kontu plāns**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-120">Create the chart of accounts on the **Chart of accounts** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-121">Galvenos kontus var izveidot lapā **Kontu plāns** vai **Galvenie konti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-121">Main accounts can be created from the **Chart of accounts** page or the **Main accounts** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-122">Galvenajos kontos nevajadzētu izmantot speciālās rakstzīmes, ko izmanto kā kontu plāna norobežotājus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-122">Your main accounts shouldn't use any special characters that are used as chart of accounts delimiters.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-123">Ja pastāv speciālā rakstzīme, kas tiek izmantota arī kā kontu plāna norobežotājs, ievadot konta un dimensijas kombinācijas, var veidoties nestabilitāte, vai arī būs nepieciešams vienmēr izmantot uzmeklēšanas rezultātus vai izlidošanu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-123">If you do have a special character that is the same as your chart of accounts delimiter, you may experience instability, or the need to always use lookups or the flyout when entering account and dimension combinations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-124">Papildinformāciju skatiet šeit: [Izveidot galveno kontu](tasks/create-main-account.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-124">For more information, see [Create a main account](tasks/create-main-account.md).</span></span>


<span data-ttu-id="54914-125">Ir ieteicams saistīt galvenos kontus ar galvenā konta kategorijām, lai varētu izmantot noklusējuma finanšu pārskatus, neveicot jebkādas izmaiņas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-125">It's a good idea to link the main accounts to main account categories, so that you can take advantage of the default financial reports without having to make any modifications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-126">Tādējādi varat ātrāk un vieglāk izstrādāt un uzturēt pārskatus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-126">Therefore, you can more quickly and easily design and maintain reports.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="54914-127">Izmantojiet lapu **Konfigurēt kontu struktūras**, lai izveidotu kontu struktūras.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-127">Use the **Configure account structures** page to create account structures.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-128">Kontu struktūras nosaka derīgās kombinācijas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-128">Account structures define valid combinations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="54914-129">Kombinācijas kopā ar galvenajiem kontiem izveido kontu plānu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-129">The combinations, together with main accounts, form a chart of accounts.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="54914-130">Papildinformāciju skatiet šeit: [Izveidot kontu struktūras](tasks/create-account-structures.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="54914-130">For more information, see [Create account structures](tasks/create-account-structures.md).</span></span>
