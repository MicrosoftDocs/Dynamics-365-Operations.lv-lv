[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="39da0-104">Laika logus varat izmantot, lai optimizētu pakalpojuma pasūtījumu rindu plānošanas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="39da0-104">You can use time windows to optimize the scheduling of service order lines.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="39da0-105">Noklikšķiniet uz **Pakalpojumu pārvaldība** \> **Iestatījumi** \> **Pakalpojumu līgumi** \> **Laika logs**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="39da0-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Time window**.</span></span>
