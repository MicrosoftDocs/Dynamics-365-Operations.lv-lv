[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="f298e-104">Izveidojot abonementa papildmaksu, jūs iestatāt abonementu pārdošanas cenas indeksu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f298e-104">You set up the index for a subscription sales price when you create a subscription fee.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f298e-105">Veidlapā **Izveidot abonementa maksu** iestatiet laukā **Iegūt cenu noteikšanu no** vērtību **Indeksēta pamatcena** un pēc tam reiziniet pamatcenu ar procentu likmi laukā **Procentuālās cenu izmaiņas**, lai saņemtu abonementa darbības pārdošanas cenu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f298e-105">In the **Create subscription fee** form, set the **Get pricing from** field to **Indexed base price**, and then multiply the base price by the percentage in the **Percent price change** field to get the sales price of the subscription transaction.</span></span>
