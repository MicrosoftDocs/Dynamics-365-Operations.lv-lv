[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="85a1a-104">Veiciet šīs darbības, lai pārvietotu pakalpojuma pasūtījumu uz nākamo posmu pakalpojuma darbplūsmā vai mainītu to atpakaļ uz iepriekšējo posmu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85a1a-104">Follow these steps to move a service order to the next stage in the service workflow or revert it to a previous stage.</span></span>
