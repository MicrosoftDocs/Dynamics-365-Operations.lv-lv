[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="7f3bb-104">Pakalpojumu intervāls norāda biežumu, ar kādu pakalpojuma pasūtījuma rindas tiek izveidotas pakalpojumu līguma rindām, kad veidojat pakalpojumu pasūtījumus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7f3bb-104">Service interval indicates the frequency with which service order lines are created for service agreement lines when you create service orders.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="7f3bb-105">Noklikšķiniet uz **Pakalpojumu pārvaldība** \> **Iestatījumi** \> **Pakalpojumu līgumi** \> **Pakalpojumu intervāli**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7f3bb-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service intervals**.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="7f3bb-108">Lauka **Diapazons** atlasiet diapazonu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7f3bb-108">In the **Range** field, select the range.</span></span>
