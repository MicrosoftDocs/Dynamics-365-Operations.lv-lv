[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="8168b-104">Noklikšķiniet uz **Pakalpojumu pārvaldība** \> **Vispārīgi** \> **Pakalpojumu līgumi** \> **Pakalpojumu līgumi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8168b-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8168b-105">Atlasiet pakalpojumu līgumu, no kura vēlaties dzēst pakalpojuma materiālu komplektu (MK).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8168b-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="8168b-107">Veidlapā **Pakalpojumu objekti** atlasiet pakalpojumu objektu, no kura vēlaties dzēst pakalpojumu MK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8168b-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8168b-108">Noklikšķiniet uz **Funkcijas** un pēc tam atlasiet **Dzēst pakalpojumu MK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8168b-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>
