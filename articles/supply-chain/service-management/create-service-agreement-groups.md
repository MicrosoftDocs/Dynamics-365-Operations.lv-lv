# <a name="create-service-agreement-groups"></a><span data-ttu-id="3a056-103">Pakalpojumu līguma grupu izveide</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-103">Create service agreement groups</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="3a056-104">Pakalpojumu līgumu grupas is noderīgas pakalpojumu līgumu kārtošanai un filtrēšanai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="3a056-105">Noklikšķiniet uz **Pakalpojumu pārvaldība** \> **Iestatījumi** \> **Pakalpojumu līgumi** \> **Pakalpojumu līgumu grupas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreement groups**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="3a056-106">Nospiediet taustiņu kombināciju **Alt+N**, lai izveidotu jaunu pakalpojuma līguma grupu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-106">Press **Alt+N** to create a new service agreement group.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="3a056-107">Laukā **Pakalpojumu līgumu grupa** ievadiet pakalpojuma līguma grupas ID.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-107">In the **Service agreement group** field, enter an ID for the service agreement group.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="3a056-108">Laukā **Apraksts** ievadiet pakalpojuma līguma grupas aprakstu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-108">In the **Description** field, enter a description for the service agreement group.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="3a056-109">Aizveriet lapu, lai saglabātu izmaiņas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-109">Close the page to save your changes.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="3a056-110">Ja pakalpojuma līguma grupa ir definēta lapas **Pakalpojumu pārvaldības parametri** laukā **Pakalpojuma līguma grupa**, programma iesaka šo grupu visiem jaunajiem pakalpojumu līgumiem, ko veidojat.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a056-110">If a service agreement group is defined in the **Service agreement group** field of the **Service management parameters** page, the program suggests this group for all new service agreements that you create.</span></span>
