<span data-ttu-id="d198f-104">Šajā tēmā ir parādīts, kā izveidot piegādes grafiku pirkšanas pasūtījumam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-104">This topic demonstrates how to create a delivery schedule for a purchase order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-105">Piegādes grafiks tiek izmantots, kad pasūtījumā vai žurnālā minēto daudzumu ir pieprasīts piegādāt vairākos sūtījumos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-105">A delivery schedule is used when a quantity on an order or a journal is requested to be delivered in multiple shipments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-106">Šajā ceļvedī rādīto piemēru var izmantot demonstrācijas datus uzņēmumā USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-106">The example shown in this guide can be used in the USMF demo data company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-107">Parasti šo procedūru veic pirkšanas aģents.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-107">This procedure would typically be done by a purchasing agent.</span></span>

## <a name="create-a-delivery-schedule"></a><span data-ttu-id="d198f-108">Piegādes grafika izveide</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-108">Create a delivery schedule</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-109">Navigācijas rūtī dodieties uz **Moduļi > Sagāde un avoti > Pirkšanas pieprasījumi > Visi pirkšanas pieprasījumi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-109">In the navigation pane, go to **Modules > Procurement and sourcing > Purchase orders > All purchase orders**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-110">Darbību rūtī atlasiet **Jauns**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-110">In the action pane, select **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-111">Laukā **Kreditora konts** ievadiet `US-101`.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-111">In the **Vendor account** field, enter `US-101`.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-112">Atlasiet **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-112">Select **OK**.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-113">Laukā **Krājuma kods** ievadiet `M0001`.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-113">In the **Item number** field, enter `M0001`.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-114">Laukā **Daudzums** ievadiet `10`.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-114">In the **Quantity** field, enter `10`.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-115">Atlasiet **Pirkšanas pasūtījuma rinda**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-115">Select **Purchase order line**.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-116">Atlasiet **Piegādes grafiks**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-116">Select **Delivery schedule**.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-117">Lapā **Piegādes grafiks** jūs varat norādīt sūtījumu skaitu, kādā no kreditora tiks piegādāts viss pasūtījuma rindas daudzums.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-117">The **Delivery schedule** page allows you to specify the number of shipments in which the total quantity of the order line will be delivered from the vendor.</span></span>  
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-118">Pēc noklusējuma sistēma no sākotnējās pirkšanas rindas uz pirmo piegādes grafika rindu kopē kopējo daudzumu un citu piegādes informāciju.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-118">By default, the system copies the total quantity and other delivery details of the original purchase line into the first delivery schedule line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-119">Šajā piemērā mēs izveidosim grafiku diviem sūtījumiem, un otrā sūtījuma datumam no pirmā sūtījuma būs nobīde par vienu nedēļu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-119">In this example, we'll create a schedule for two shipments, with the second shipment's date offset by a week from the first shipment.</span></span>  
9. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-120">Laukā **Daudzums** mainiet daudzumu uz `4`.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-120">In the **Quantity** field, change the quantity to `4`.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-121">Atlasiet **Jauns**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-121">Select **New**.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-122">Laukā **Daudzums** ievadiet `6` kā atlikušo daudzumu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-122">In the **Quantity** field, enter `6` as the remaining quantity.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-123">Piegādes datuma laukā atlasiet datumu, kas ir vienu nedēļu pēc pirmās piegādes rindas datuma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-123">In the delivery date field, select a date that's one week after the date on the first delivery line.</span></span>  
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-124">Piegādes grafika rindām piešķirtajam kopējam daudzumam varat sekot līdzi, apskatot laukus **Kopsumma** un **Atlicis**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-124">You can keep track of the total quantity that's allocated to the delivery schedule lines by looking at the **Total** and **Remaining** fields.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-125">Ja atlikušais daudzums ir nulle, grafikam ir piešķirts viss daudzums no sākotnējās rindas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-125">When the remaining quantity is zero, the full quantity from the original line has been allocated to the schedule.</span></span>  
12. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-126">Izvērsiet sadaļu **Izmaksu konvertēšana**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-126">Expand the **Charges conversion** section.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-127">Šeit pieejamās opcijas jums ļauj kontrolēt veidu, kādā maksas vēlaties sadalīt pa piegādes grafika rindām.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-127">The options here allow you to control how you want charges to be distributed across the delivery schedule lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-128">Ja atlasāt **Kopēt bruto summas**, tad uz katru piegādes rindu tiek kopēta sākotnējās pasūtījuma rindas maksas summa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-128">If you select **Copy gross amounts**, the charge amount on the original order line is copied to each delivery line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-129">Opcija **Piešķirt piegādes rindām** sākotnējās rindas maksu sadala atbilstoši daudzumam katrā piegādes rindā.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-129">The **Allocate to delivery lines** option divides the original line charge according to the quantity on each delivery line.</span></span>  
13. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-130">Sakļaujiet sadaļu **Izmaksu konvertēšana**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-130">Collapse the **Charges conversion** section.</span></span>
14. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-131">Atlasiet **Labi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-131">Select **OK**.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-132">Tagad piegādes grafiks ir lietots pasūtījumam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-132">The delivery schedule has now been applied to the order.</span></span>  
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-133">Sākotnējā pasūtījuma rinda, ko tagad sauc par komerciālo rindu, ir pārvērsta par pasūtījuma rindu ar vairākām piegādēm.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-133">The original order line, now referred to as a Commercial line, has been converted to an Order line with multiple deliveries.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-134">Tā ir atzīmēta ar atšķirīgu ikonu un darbojas kā piegādes rindu virsraksts.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-134">It is marked with a distinct icon and acts as a header for the delivery lines.</span></span>  
15. <span data-ttu-id="d198f-135">Atlasiet otro pasūtījuma rindu, kas ir pirmā no abām piegādes rindām.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-135">Select the second order line, which is the first of the two delivery lines.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="d198f-136">Abas jaunās rindas, sauktas par piegādes rindām, veido vienu piegādes grafiku.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-136">The two new lines, referred to as Delivery lines, make up one delivery schedule.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-137">Pasūtījums tiks apstrādāts pret šīm rindām, nevis sākotnējo rindu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-137">The order will be processed against these lines and not the original line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d198f-138">Ja tiek drukāti dokumenti, piemēram, apstiprinājumi, produktu ieejas plūsmu žurnāli vai rēķini, tiek rādītas tikai piegādes rindas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d198f-138">If documents such as confirmations, product receipt journals, or invoices are printed, only the delivery lines are shown.</span></span>
